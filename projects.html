A.I. generated Batman hell

Summary:

 An A.I. project to develop an understanding of Tools within our Kafe for accessible game development. Researchers will discover applications techniques and backend knowledge, application operators to work on experiment and test A.I. applications, procedural map generation and mechanics related to 3d game design. Part of this project might include finding parameters that can be manipulated in Python from game design software and implementing A.I. generation functions/ parameters.

maybe isometric procedural generation

Part of the objective is to make program operation easily accessible and comprehensible  within the Kafe even though these may be complex topics. Be prepared to share your thinking casually and work with potentially silly people(and carry yourself well).
We prioritize support appreciation humility and humour for this project.

Video Edit Funniez Kompilation- [TOPIC FUSION]

Summary:

For people who use video editing software or want to learn it and contribute. For this project, everyone involved gets a humor topic to focus on for the week(or bi-week) and the objective is to make a video of a few seconds to be compiled into a collaborative 5 minute video at the end and uploaded to a social media platform with no particular purpose.



Justification:

Its very easy to make/ edit video content. The resulting video should have a unique appeal which exhibits everyones style. While excecuting this project, we can learn and be inspired from others video editing style and see what they are good at. This familiarity can be used for more serious video editing projects in the future.
