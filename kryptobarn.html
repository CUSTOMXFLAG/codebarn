CRYPTO FARM

Inspired by the cockpit of Star Trek with no Captain Kirk, this is a collaborative penny trading project that  incorporates gaming and multimedia communication into casual voice chats.

From Black Jack to Elden Ring, to Krunker, to Mobile Games, to Film and Sports, integrating social and gaming applications with real money. Finding monetary value in gaming and digital organized communication is part of our loose objectives.
