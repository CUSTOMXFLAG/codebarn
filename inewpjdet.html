Planning process:

1. MEETING: CONCEPT

2. SECOND MEETING(LONG) - CASUAL UNDIRECTED

3. ANONYMOUS (Y/N)VOTE PROCESS.

Decision leads to planning meeting or discarding the project. and everyone will brainstorm 
-brainstorming/ reflection period

4. Planning meeting: notes are made and loose plans are established which will get revised later if its decided worthy of revision by the entire panel.

5. Planning reiteration meeting:everyone brings their brainstormed thoughts for the project at the presentation.

6.In some cases we will adopt plans based on a point system of y/n but we encourage everyone to make great consideration in their decision

Then well do more closer planning after monthly or bi monthly mandatory meetings
(The person/entity who thought of the project will be absent from part of this process for bias reasons, but will be granted a top role if the project is taken up.)

4. Special donuts meeting- Long casual meeting where the objective is free expression of thought before we move forward


Our approach:

Characterized by having "low" standards primarily self directed and recreational, prioritizing community cohesion sharing ideas and techniques and thought comfortability

If you express aggression abuse or lack of apparent comfortability, you can be exiled or removed by the project by the decision of the panel.

Everyone is their own boss but will contribute genuinely based on what their interests are in contributing to any project

We dont have high expectations and focus on valuable work with genuine interest


Conditions:

Everyone should contribute genuinely while intoxicated in some way
if you act too serious you can be thrown off the project
- Everyone has to read the mandatory material (if any)
* Mandatory material will be related to wider context or technical knowledge


Ethic:

Although everyone has their own views we discourage serious conversations about some topics because we want to harbour healthy positive collaboration:
not political
not religious
not racist
unsafe drug/ pornography/food use

--THIS IS A POSITIVE COLLABORATION WORK ENVIRONMENT
--if you have un-merited run along sentences- you can be thrown off
-- if you explain things in ways that nobody can understand or are otherwise making your communication lesser accessible, you can be thrown off 
-- if you are way off deadlines(that are designed for , you can be thrown off
